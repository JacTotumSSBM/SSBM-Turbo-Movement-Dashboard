Melee · Ground Movement

Frame Data Console

Interactive re-implementation of the ground-movement velocity formulas (dash, foxtrot, wavedash, dash dance) from schmooblidon's "Ultimate Ground Movement Analysis" thread on smashboards.com. Character constants come from that thread's reference tables; dash, foxtrot, wavedash, and dash dance figures are computed live from those constants using the same per-frame formulas. Extended dash dance figures are sourced directly from the thread's published tables. Click any property on the left to rank all 26 characters by it on the right.
Melee-metre (Mm) ≈ half a foot. Perfect waveland airdodge v = 2.79000  |  Max angle v = 2.67039  |  Diagonal notch v = 1.97283. Sheik, Marth, Link, DK, Roy and Bowser wavedash figures are corrected for their non-standard airdodge trajectory (−0.5×w on max angle / diagonal notch).
